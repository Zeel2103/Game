Welcome to a game of hangman

How to play

  • To play the game you must first login if you do not have an account you can register your details on the register page and then login using those details.
  • Once logged in you can select the theme you would like to play and then press the button to get a new word according to the theme.
  • The aim of the game is to guess the word and you are allowed up to five incorrect guesses, once that is reached you have lost, however you can press the get new word button to play again by generating a new word.
  • The words you guess correctly will be added to your score and your score can be seen on the rankings page.
